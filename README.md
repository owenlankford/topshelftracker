## The Game That Started It All

I've been playing lacrosse since 4th grade. I still remember the first time I picked up a stick — the weight of it, the way the mesh cradled the ball, the feeling that I'd found something I was going to love for a long time. I was right.

Over the years lacrosse became more than just a sport to me. It shaped how I think about competition, teamwork, and putting in work when no one is watching. From youth leagues to playing through high school, the game has always been a constant. There's something about the speed of it — the quick passes, the dodges, the way a well-placed shot hits the back of the net — that never gets old no matter how many years you've been playing.

If you've played the sport you know exactly what I'm talking about. And if you haven't, I genuinely feel like you're missing out.

---

## Why I Built This

As a lifelong lacrosse player and fan, I was frustrated with how hard it was to follow scores across all three NCAA divisions in one place. D1 gets decent coverage, but D2 and D3? You're usually hunting across multiple websites, refreshing outdated pages, or just missing games entirely.

I wanted a single dashboard where I could pull up any division, filter by gender, search for a specific team, and see everything — live games, upcoming matchups, and past results — all in one clean view. Something fast, no ads, no fluff.

So I built it myself.

TopShelfTracker pulls live data from the ESPN and NCAA APIs, aggregates it server-side, and serves it instantly. It covers all three NCAA divisions for both Men's and Women's lacrosse throughout the entire season. Whether you're a player, a coach, a parent in the stands, or just a fan of the game, this is the dashboard I always wished existed.

## Features

- 🔴 **Live scores** with real-time game clock and period
- 🕐 **Upcoming games** rail with scheduled times
- ✅ **Full season results** with pagination
- 🔍 **Team search** with autocomplete — division-specific
- ⚡ **Instant loading** via server-side aggregation and client caching
- 📱 **Mobile friendly** — works on any device
- 🏷️ **D1 / D2 / D3** — all three divisions, Men's and Women's
- 🔄 **Auto-refresh** every 60 seconds during live games
